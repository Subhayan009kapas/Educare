Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking is the practice of identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities in computer systems and networks in order to improve their security. Ethical hackers, also known as white hat hackers, use the same methods and tools as malicious hackers, but with the owner's permission and with the goal of improving the security of the system. The goal of ethical hacking is to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in computer systems and networks before malicious hackers can exploit them.
